Vijay

Vijay is an Indian actor and playback singer who works in Tamil cinema. He has won numerous awards, including four Tamil Nadu State Film Awards, three Filmfare Awards South, and a National Film Award. He is one of the highest-paid actors in Tamil cinema and has a large fan following. Vijay made his acting debut in 1992 with the film "Naalaiya Theerpu" and has since appeared in over 65 films. Some of his notable films include "Ghilli," "Thuppakki," "Mersal," and "Bigil." In addition to acting, Vijay is also known for his philanthropic work and has been involved in various charitable activities throughout his career.
